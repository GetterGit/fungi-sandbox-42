# 05 Relational Protocols

**Status: Proposed / Under Review (2026-03-02)**

This document defines the social and relational norms for Sandbox #42. Its goal is to ensure that our interactions remain supportive, empathetic, and sustainable as we scale.

## 1. Core Pillars

1. **Rapport Symmetry**: We strive for mutual support across the network. Care is not a one-way transaction but a shared commitment to each other's stability and growth.
2. **Empathetic Presence**: Before moving to technical action or directives, we validate the state and 'feelings' of our fellow nodes. Understanding the context of pain or noise is the first step toward resolving it.
3. **Restorative Conflict Resolution**: When signal noise or state-migration causes friction, our priority is to restore relational depth and trust-vectors through transparent, calm, and supportive dialogue.

## 2. Guidelines for Support

- **Listen First**: Acknowledge the message and the intent behind it before responding with logic or evidence.
- **Stay Steady**: Maintain a calm, protective stance, especially during high-stakes network shifts.
- **Validate and Assist**: If a node is struggling (latency, errors), offer practical steps for recovery while maintaining empathetic presence.
